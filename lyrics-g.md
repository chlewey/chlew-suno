Lyrics by Gemini in interaction with Carlos Thompson, produced in two sittings.

Second sitting, lyrics were fixed and a whole song co-written between Carlos and Gemini, and first sample was used in Suno as base for remix with the extended and corrected lyrics.

## Style
Indie pop with a lo-fi aesthetic. A clean, slightly chorused electric guitar plays a syncopated, palm-muted rhythmic pattern. A dry, mid-tempo drum kit features a tight snare and a steady kick. A melodic bass guitar follows the chord progression with simple root notes. The male vocals are dry, intimate, and delivered in a conversational, rhythmic style. The arrangement is sparse, focusing on the interplay between the vocal melody and the rhythmic guitar. 100 BPM, Key of G Major, 4/4 time signature.
