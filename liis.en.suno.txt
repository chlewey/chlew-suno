[Verse 1]
She saw him first in the crowded noon light,
Salt passed between strangers, words taking flight.
Forty-six and unrushed, a man out of time,
Colombian stories wrapped in Swedish rhyme.
He moved like the world was rooms he could choose,
She watched from the edge, quiet and curious.

[Pre-Chorus]
No rescue, no future, no need to be more,
Just a feeling that flickered along her shore.

[Chorus]
Liis, Liis, walking streets made of frost,
Heart like an Estonian winter, beautiful but lost.
Thomas was interesting in the way an unopened door is,
She stepped inside for a season, then left it just as it is.
Liis, Liis, never asked him to stay.

[Verse 2]
His hands knew the language experience brings,
Attentive, unhurried, he never pulled strings.
She liked how he didn’t pretend it was love,
Just warm skin and silence in the dark months above.
But she noticed the phone and the names in his eyes,
The women who mattered, the ones he disguised.

[Pre-Chorus]
She let him believe she was simple and light,
A girl for the dark nights, a temporary delight.

[Chorus]
Liis, Liis, walking streets made of frost,
Heart like an Estonian winter, beautiful but lost.
Thomas was interesting in the way an unopened door is,
She stepped inside for a season, then left it just as it is.
Liis, Liis, never asked him to stay.

[Bridge]
He saw a landscape, a twenty-two-year-old scene,
She was a city he visited but never quite dreamed.
Tõnis was safety, Thomas was view,
She took what she needed and quietly withdrew.

[Verse 3]
Now the year fades like snow into rain,
No disappointment, no lingering pain.
She was a window he looked through, briefly aware,
While she saw the weight that he carried with care.
Two different stories, one quiet goodbye.

[Pre-Chorus]
No promises broken, no tears left to cry.

[Chorus / Outro]
Liis, Liis, walking streets made of frost,
Heart like an Estonian winter, beautiful but lost.
Thomas was interesting in the way an unopened door is,
She stepped inside for a season… then left without goodbyes.
Liis, Liis… never asked him to stay.
